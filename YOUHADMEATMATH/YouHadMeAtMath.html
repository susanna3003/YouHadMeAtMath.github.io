You Had Me At Math! Many children struggle with comprehending the beginner basics of mathematics and can't always get individual focused assistance in a large classroom. At You Had Me At Math, we care about the academic progress of our students and have created a fun and innovative way of letting children tutor themselves at home. With useful and interactive features like linked parent-student accounts to let parents monitor progress and customizable math quizzes to test yourself at your own pace.
